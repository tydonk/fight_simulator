How To

Instructions

  1. Start by selecting a gender. Fights can only be between the same gender, but fighters may challenge opponents from different promotions and weight classes. Fighters cannot fight each other.
  2. Once you select a gender all the available fighters will appear in the fighter list. You may filter the fighter list further by selection a promotion. Choosing a promotion will load the weight classes available in that organization.
  3. When you have a fighter selected in each corner, click the "FIGHT!" button to start a simulation. Each corner has a reset button if you want to clear your selection before the fight.
  4. The results panel for the fight will appear at the bottom of the page.
  5. After your fight has been simulated, use the 'Start Over' button to simulate another matchup.
  6. Logging in will automatically save each of your fights in your user history.
